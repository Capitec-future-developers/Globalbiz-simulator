Why the difference?
Your Available Balance is how much you may spend. The Balance could differ from the Available Balance because:

• R150 is reserved in your account as this is the minimum account balance required to have a GlobalBiz account (Refer to our fees for further information).
• Your account might have transactions that are reserved on your card but have not yet cleared.
• In the case of a negative balance, you may have outstanding fees that must be settled before you can continue transacting. Close
